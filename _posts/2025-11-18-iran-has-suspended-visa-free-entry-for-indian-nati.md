  Iran has suspended visa-free entry for Indian nationals for travel and
  transit. 


  From 19th November, Indian nationals with passports will now be required to
  obtain a visa to enter or transit through the territory of the Islamic
  Republic of Iran: Iran Statement. 


  Indian nationals previously had the privilege of entering Iran without a visa
  for 15 days, a privilege that has now been revoked.
