  Uber to invest $200 million in new tech hub in Istanbul, its fourth outside
  the US


  Uber plans to invest $200 million over the next five years to establish a
  software and technology development center in Istanbul, Turkiye, the country’s
  Industry and Technology Ministry announced on Friday.


  According to the ministry, this will be Uber’s fourth center outside the US,
  joining existing facilities in Brazil, India, and the Netherlands.
